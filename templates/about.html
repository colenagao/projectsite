My journey into software

Building things has always been my passion, from building legos as a child, to woodworking and 3D printing in my youth. I love the process of creating something from nothing, and the satisfaction of seeing a project come to life. I discovered my love of programming in high school, and have been hooked ever since. Now with my B.S. in Computer Science I hope to continue building and creating projects that will help people have a healthy and positive relationship with technology.
