Virtual Pet Calendar

Virtual Pet Calendar is a web application that allows users to keep track of their pet's health and happiness. Users can create a pet, feed it, play with it, and take it to the vet. The pet's health and happiness levels are displayed on a calendar, and users can see how their pet is doing at a glance. The application uses HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Firebase for the backend.

Learned how to use Oauth2.0 paired with googles SSO in order to access calendar and task data from google calendar. It was also just a fun playful project that was inspired by a necessity of motivation and obligation to be productive
