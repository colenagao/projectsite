Virtual Pet Calendar

Virtual Pet Calendar is a web application that allows users to keep track of their pet's health and happiness. Users can create a pet, feed it, play with it, and take it to the vet. The pet's health and happiness levels are displayed on a calendar, and users can see how their pet is doing at a glance. The application uses HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Firebase for the backend.

Problem Space

In today's fast-paced world, many individuals struggle with staying motivated and maintaining productivity. Traditional calendar and task management apps, while effective, often lack engagement and a sense of obligation, making it easy for users to ignore or procrastinate on their responsibilities.

The Virtual Pet Calendar was designed to gamify productivity by integrating task management with an interactive virtual pet. The pet’s well-being depends on the user’s ability to complete scheduled tasks and manage their time effectively. By creating a playful and rewarding system, this project aimed to bridge the gap between responsibility and motivation, encouraging users to stay consistent with their schedules in a fun, engaging way.

This project space sits at the intersection of productivity tools, behavioral psychology, and gamification, leveraging technology to transform habit-building into an enjoyable and interactive experience.
